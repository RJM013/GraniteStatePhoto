HOW IT WORKS

A straightforward process from first email to delivered files—so your staff knows what to expect.

Planning & session flow

We align with your coach or athletic director on schedule, location, and shot list—individuals, captains, position groups, or full team as needed. Athletes get clear direction on set so lines move smoothly and nobody is left guessing.

Editing & consistency

We adjust exposure, color, and contrast so the set feels cohesive—same background treatment, same skin tones, same punch—whether you’re using one image or the whole roster.

Files you can hand off

You receive high-resolution digital files suitable for large prints, programs, and posters, plus web-friendly sizes when you need them for sites and social. Use them with your designer or upload directly—no mystery formats.
