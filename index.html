Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photography services

How do I book a photography session?

Booking a session is easy! Simply use our contact form to book us directly. We'll discuss your needs, select a date that works for both of us, and confirm your booking with a deposit.

What areas of New Hampshire do you serve?

We're based in Strafford, NH but serve clients throughout the Granite State. We regularly work in Portsmouth, Manchester, Concord, Lake Winnipesaukee area, and the White Mountains. For locations farther away, a travel fee may apply.

How long until I receive my photos?

For standard sessions, you'll receive your private online gallery within 2 weeks. For sports events and larger sessions, it may take up to 3 weeks. Rush processing is available for an additional fee if you need your photos sooner.

What happens if there's bad weather on the day of our outdoor session?

New Hampshire weather can be unpredictable! If there's inclement weather on your session day, we'll reschedule to the next available date that works for both of us. We keep a close eye on forecasts and will be in touch 24-48 hours before your session if weather looks concerning.
