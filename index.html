About Granite State Photo

Granite State Photo was founded with a deep appreciation for New Hampshire's natural splendor and the families who call it home. From the laughter of children playing in autumn leaves to the serene beauty of the White Mountains, our mission is to preserve your most cherished moments.

With 2 years of experience and even more passion, we bring both technical expertise and a heartfelt understanding of what makes a moment worth remembering. Our approach blends candid photography with thoughtful composition to create images that tell your unique story.

As proud New Hampshire natives, we intimately know the hidden gems and scenic vistas that make the perfect backdrop for your portraits through all four of our beautiful seasons.

Our sports photography captures the intensity, determination, and triumph of athletic achievements. From high school championships to youth leagues, we're there to document those split-second moments that athletes and their families will treasure for years to come.
