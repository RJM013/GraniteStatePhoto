Senior Portrait Approach

A simple, fun process that highlights personality, school pride, and what matters most

Your Story, Your Way

Senior year only happens once. Let’s celebrate it with photos that tell your story. Whether you want something timeless, bold, or full of personality, we’ll design a session that captures the real you. From yearbook shots to creative outdoor portraits, this is about creating images that make you proud now, and years from now.

Outfits and Styling Made Easy

Want images that feel like you? Bring 2 to 3 outfits and we will help coordinate looks and locations. Clean yearbook options, casual styles, and a standout look are all planned around your personality so your gallery feels authentic and confident.

Guided Poses for a Comfortable Flow

We bring a binder of poses to keep things moving naturally and help you feel at ease. Clear, simple direction ensures you look relaxed and confident while still feeling like yourself in every shot.

How Your Senior Session Works

Clear, stress‑free steps from booking to a gallery you love

1

Consultation & Booking

Tell us your vibe, locations, and any yearbook requirements or deadlines. We’ll lock in a date and plan the look and feel.

2

Prep Made Simple

We’ll send a quick prep guide with outfit tips, what to bring, and timeline details so you show up confident and ready.

3

Your Session

Relaxed, guided posing with natural expressions. Sessions run ~1–2 hours with time for multiple looks and locations, depending on your package.

4

Select & Share

Within two weeks, you'll receive a link to your private gallery to review, choose your favorites, and download professionally retouched images for your yearbook, social media, and prints.
