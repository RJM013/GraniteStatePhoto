New Hampshire Through the Seasons

Experience the ever-changing beauty of the Granite State as it transforms through spring renewal, summer vibrancy, autumn splendor, and winter serenity.

Spring Renewal

As snow melts and temperatures rise, New Hampshire comes alive with vibrant greens, blooming wildflowers, and rushing waterfalls. Spring brings a sense of renewal to the landscape and offers unique photography opportunities as nature awakens.

Our spring sessions capture the fresh energy of the season, from delicate tree blossoms to mist-covered mountains. It's the perfect time for family portraits among flowering trees or capturing children surrounded by new life.

Best Spring Photography Locations:

  • Flume Gorge - dramatic spring runoff and emerging greenery
  • Lake Winnipesaukee - morning mist rising from the water
  • Lupine fields in Sugar Hill - typically blooming in early June

Summer Adventure

Summer in New Hampshire brings lush green landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and endless outdoor activities. With longer days and warm temperatures, it's the perfect time to capture families enjoying the natural playgrounds of the Granite State.

Our summer sessions focus on active lifestyles, lakeside moments, and the vibrant energy of the season. Whether it's sunset beach portraits, mountaintop family photos, or capturing children's summer adventures, this season offers endless creative possibilities.

Best Summer Photography Locations:

  • Hampton Beach - golden hour family portraits on the sand
  • Mount Major - sunset hikes with panoramic views
  • Echo Lake - swimming and beach activities beneath Cathedral Ledge

Fall Splendor

New Hampshire's fall foliage is world-renowned, transforming the landscape into a breathtaking canvas of reds, oranges, and golds. This magical time brings photographers from around the world to capture the state's most iconic season.

Our fall sessions are our most popular, placing families against the backdrop of nature's magnificent color show. From apple orchards to forest paths carpeted with leaves, autumn provides rich, warm tones that create unforgettable family portraits.

Best Fall Photography Locations:

  • Kancamagus Highway - panoramic views of colorful mountains
  • Mack's Apples in Londonderry - family apple picking sessions
  • Franconia Notch - reflected fall colors in Echo Lake

Winter Serenity

Winter transforms New Hampshire into a serene wonderland of snow-covered mountains, frozen lakes, and peaceful forests. The stark beauty of this season offers dramatic landscapes and cozy moments that make for stunning photography.

Our winter sessions capture the quiet magic of the season, from family snowball fights to serene portraits in freshly fallen snow. The brilliant white backdrops and early sunsets create a special atmosphere that's unlike any other time of year.

Best Winter Photography Locations:

  • Mount Washington - dramatic snow-covered vistas
  • Jackson - covered bridges in winter settings
  • Prescott Park in Portsmouth - holiday lights and winter decorations
