The Portrait Experience

What to expect when booking a portrait session with us

1

Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, needs, and how you'll use your portraits. This helps us plan the perfect session tailored to your goals.

2

Preparation

You'll receive a detailed preparation guide with clothing suggestions, styling tips, and session information. We're available to answer any questions as you prepare for your portrait day.

3

The Session

On session day, we'll guide you through flattering poses and expressions, making you comfortable in front of the camera. Sessions typically last 1-2 hours depending on the package.

4

Selection & Delivery

Within two weeks, you'll receive access to your online gallery where you can select your favorite images. After selection, we'll professionally retouch your final portraits and deliver them in private digital gallery.
